My work

Introduction

Project A is a hack-and-slash arena game from BAG Studios. I am working on it as a co-founder, with a focus on gameplay systems and combat feel.

The goal is to make each encounter easy to read, satisfying to control, and simple for the team to tune as the game grows.

Combat systems

I am focusing on player-facing systems: attack timing, impact, feedback, and the small details that make fast combat feel reliable instead of messy.

The systems need to support responsive actions while staying clear enough for enemies, arenas, and future mechanics to build on.

Enemy pressure

A big part of the work is making enemy pressure readable. The player should feel surrounded and challenged, but not confused by random behavior.

I am keeping the implementation simple to tune so the team can quickly test enemy behavior, pacing, and encounter changes.

What I am building toward

Project A is still in development, so the most important thing right now is building a clean foundation: systems that feel good now and do not fight the team later.
