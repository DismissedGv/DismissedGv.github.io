Wratful Hedgehog

About

Wrathful Hedgehog was our first game project at FutureGames.

The game was built with C# and Unity together with a team of 14 people assigned by FutureGames.

Info

Role: AI & Gameplay Programmer

Team Size: 14

Time: 2 Months

Engine: Unity

Introduction

In this project I took the responsibility of programming the game's gameplay and artificial intelligence since that was our second core mechanic of the game. We use Perforce desktop as source control, Miro for prototyping, Jira for project management. I mainly put lot of focus on AI but I also worked making the cave shrink, health, and UI. I also wanted to get a better understanding of unity AI since it was my first time using it.

Gameplay

Health and UI was basic, whenever the player was taking damage I had a collider function on the enemie when they were in attacking state and collided with the player.

The game manager was used for knowing when the round was over. there were 2 turns, one for the when the enemies were spawning in and the cave shrinking and one for where the player could get ready for the next wave after defeating all the enemies in the previous round.

Artificial Intelligence

This was my first time doing any artificial intelligence, therefore nothing too complicated was made in this project.

I installed Unity's AI Navigation to utilize a NavMesh for ground surface navigation and a NavMesheshAgent for enemy pathfinding, enabling them to navigate around obstacles.

I also implemented the enemy spawner, which communicates with the game manager to determine when and how many enemies to spawn. The enemy script handles their individual behaviors, including health management, movement, attacking, and targeting the player.

What I learned

Working on this project with someone other than myself taught me a lot. I learned that it is important to not assume things, even if it seems obvious to you. After the project we also did code reviews, which was really edducational for me to see another persons coding style when it comes to game development.
