My work

Introduction

Marked For Deletion was a meta Unreal project about deleting the development team. As people disappear, parts of the game start disappearing too.

I worked across gameplay, controller setup, animation support, and the deletion systems that made the concept work.

Gameplay

I modified the third-person blueprint into a first-person controller so the prototype matched the final direction of the game.

I also refactored crew-list widget behavior into a reusable function, which made deletion effects easier to trigger and manage.

Animation

I supported character movement and presentation using Mixamo animation packs and Unreal implementation.

The goal was to keep the game readable and playable while the team moved quickly through a short production timeline.

What I learned

This project taught me how useful outside feedback can be, especially when a feature makes sense to the team but does not read clearly to players yet.
